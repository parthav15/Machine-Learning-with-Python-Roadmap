Step 9: PYTHON - Statistics

  • Probability Theory: Understanding probability distributions (Bernoulli, Binomial, Normal, Poisson), Bayes' theorem, and conditional probability.
  • Descriptive Statistics: Measures of central tendency (mean, median, mode) and variability (range, variance, standard deviation).
  • Inferential Statistics: Hypothesis testing (t-tests, ANOVA), confidence intervals, and regression analysis.
  • Linear Algebra: Vector operations, matrix multiplication, eigenvalues, and eigenvectors.
  • Bayesian Statistics: Bayes' theorem, prior and posterior distributions, and Bayesian inference.
  • Time Series Analysis: Autocorrelation, partial autocorrelation, ARIMA models, and forecasting.
  • Regression Analysis: Simple and multiple linear regression, logistic regression, and regression metrics (R-squared, MSE).
  • Hypothesis Testing: Types of errors (Type I, Type II), power analysis, and common statistical tests (t-tests, ANOVA, chi-squared tests).
